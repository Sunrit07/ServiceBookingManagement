About Us

This Capstone project is developed by Sunrit Sarkar, demonstrating proficiency in full-stack development with a focus on .NET and Angular frameworks. The Service Booking Management System is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline user and service management processes. The application enables seamless registration, login, and role-based access control for both users and administrators. Users can efficiently manage their profiles, book services, view the status of their requests, and cancel appointments if necessary. Administrators are provided with a robust dashboard to oversee service requests, update statuses, and generate reports.
